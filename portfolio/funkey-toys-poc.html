FunKey Toys POC

This was a fun project that allowed me to explore a little bit in the world of UX and visual design while getting to know the Shopify platform a little better.

Case study/Research

It all starts with a Case study for my example site. Taking a look at sites that are promoting the same content as mine, and seeing how I can improve on them based on the needs of the potential users on my site. From there, I went out on a search for what the competition is doing. What works well, what doesn't work well, and why?
